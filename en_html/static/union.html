Welcome to join 1001 Dream Hotels AllianceTM

Dream Hotels AllianceTM connects excellent boutique hotels around the world, and forms a huge family where they help each other and share resources. It’s an alliance managed by hotel owners. Our hotels range from avant-garde modern hotels to lavish 17-century castles, from secret paradise in the city center to the private island far from urban life, from time-honored country house to idyllic resort. They are distinctive individuals but meanwhile a united community, which shows exactly the charm of the Alliance.

The initiating and founding member 1001HOTEL® is itself a boutique hotel. It understands that, compared to gigantic hotel groups in the world (like IHG and Starwood) which have their powerful reservation and membership system, small hotels have difficulties in marketing and branding on their own. But if various boutique hotels gather together, they will make a strong force and attract more guests.

Why did so many boutique hotels join 1001 Dream Hotels AllianceTM?

We possess thorough understanding of the domestic market. We see that Chinese tourists have strong demand for foreign boutique hotels, while the established online distribution channels are not suitable for this kind of accommodation. We thus adopt a unique business model that creates an online distribution platform for every single boutique hotel for free. 1001 Dream Hotels AllianceTM aims to precisely target the potential clients for every member so as to maximize member hotel’s revenues.

Why 1001 Dream Hotels AllianceTM？

Attentive to fundamental needs of the hotels

Since most of our guests are nature-lovers and dream-chasers, we only consider unique and dreamy boutique hotels that own 5-60 rooms. We do not accept any big hotel, business hotel, or other kinds of accommodation. You will thus never be a minimum player in the game.

Managed by hotel owners

1001Dream Hotels AllianceTM is an institution where members offer aid mutually, hence no risk of any debt. It is managed by representatives selected by member hotels, and governed by board of directors. Board members are elected by hotel owners and take the position for one year.

Allowing independence

The Alliance applies “soft” branding and marketing, which means you can not only gain the identity as a landmark international boutique hotel, but also enjoy independent ownership.

Boasting the proprietary online marketing platform

The independently developed Alliance APP offers every boutique hotel its own online distribution platform. The APP can also build for every member its own CRS system, which connects numerous hotels and allows every one to be the system’s owner. Hotels can create a marketing platform with their own style, and at the same time directly interact with guests in the world through the system. They can also make transactions on this system independently, secretly, and effectively.

High threshold and quality

High threshold and quality 1001Dream Hotels AllianceTM won its advantages and reputations from its top-notch member hotels. We thus strictly control the quality so as to ensure that only outstanding and high-standard hotels are accepted as members and they keep their excellence after joining the alliance. We believe that, when assessing the candidate hotel, customer’s overall experience is of equal importance as other factors.

Inspection scheme

We perform regular inspection to maintain 1001Dream Hotels AllianceTM’s quality, which is critical to clients, tourism industry, media, and also you – member hotels. We need to sustain our huge family’s reputation on a global scale, as the guests would demand a hotel as high quality as other ones they have experienced. We also carry on invisible client-experience inspection program, which only requires you to be responsible for the inspector’s stay in your hotel and his/her traveling expenditure between Hong Kong (our headquarter) and your place.

Hotel construction and renovation

We also welcome hotels in process, including new resorts or old buildings under renovation, to join our family. As an expert in boutique hotel, 1001Dream Hotels AllianceTM provides hotels with all-around services, encompassing technical support for hotel development and pre-opening services. We will give instructions and suggestions regarding planning, construction, and interior design, which the hotels could opt for depending on their own needs and future profitability. Our advices make sure that the hotel obtains optimal design and operates following service standard of luxurious hotels from the very beginning.
