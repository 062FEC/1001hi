公司简介

1001 HOTEL® Group holds the mission that we "realize your dream to accomplish ours"

The Group’s business covers the whole industry chain of new tourism and resort with its cutting-edge online and offline platforms. Its 1001 HOTEL® is the only green chain resort in the world that encompasses hospitality design, modular home construction, hospitality management, and hospitality operation all together. Its 1001 Dream Hotels AllianceTM connects excellent boutique hotels around the world, and forms a huge family where they help each other and share resources without losing their independent management. The Group provides services and techniques for our guests, franchisors, and hotel owners, letting them realize their own dreams in our eco-system.

品牌与故事

Origin of 1001 HOTEL

1908

The book Anne of Green Gables by Canadian writer Lucy Maud Montgomery was published, bringing world fame to the protagonist Anne and her log cabin in Prince Edward Island, Canada.

1950

Inspired by Anne’s log cabin, the Canadian Roger Faulkner and his family transformed traditional North American log cabin into a house that could be entirely moved as a unit, and thus created the outdoor retreat brand – 1001 HOTEL. Till today, Roger’s house and the brand 1001 HOTEL have existed in North America for over 60 years.

The Dream of 1001 Nights

2006

Having visited many extremely distinctive places, Kevin Hon – a Chinese traveler passionate with photographing, design and engineering material – decided to change the reality that the tourists “see the heaven while stay in the hell”, and founded 1001 Nights Tourism Investment Co., Ltd.

Brand New 1001 HOTEL, Brand New Dream

2008

Accidentally, Kevin met Roger and his 1001 HOTEL. Roger’s modular house was just the magical box Kevin had been looking for. At the same time, Roger was deeply moved by Kevin and his “1001 Nights” dream, and decided to entrust to Kevin the over 60 years’ old modular house system and the brand “1001 HOTEL” so that they could flourish in the East.

全新的1001 HOTEL正式进入亚洲

2011

Kevin and his dream team reshaped the modular house and produced a new recreation vehicle named T-box®. This creation was awarded Best RV Invention Award in Canada and many patents in China. Moreover, Kevin employed modern hospitality management and community operation thinking in the running of T-box®, bringing into being this green tourism and resort system – another legend of “1001 Nights”.
