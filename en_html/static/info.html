1001 HOTEL’s Japanese Environment Expert Mr. Yamamori Takes Interview from the Media

2015/03/25

Hong Kong, China – February 6, 2015: During the visit to its project site in Mount Xiqiao, Guangdong, the group met the press and answered questions regarding microbiological sewage treatment. In the interview, Mr. Yamamori had given detailed account on how to promote effective, simple, low-cost, and advanced domestic sewage treatment in China. He also thoroughly introduced the self-circulating water treatment system that 1001 HOTEL specifically designed for boutique hotels.
